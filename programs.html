🏊‍♂️ Swim Team

Ages 5-12 | Beginner to Intermediate

Our Swim Team program is designed for young athletes looking to develop their swimming skills in a supportive, team-oriented environment. Athletes learn proper stroke technique, build endurance, and have the opportunity to compete in local swim meets.

What You'll Learn:

  • All four competitive strokes (freestyle, backstroke, breaststroke, butterfly)
  • Proper starts and turns
  • Race strategy and pacing
  • Team sportsmanship and camaraderie

Schedule:

Practice 3x per week | Tuesday, Thursday, Saturday
Session times: 4:00 PM - 5:30 PM (weekdays), 9:00 AM - 10:30 AM (Saturday)

🤽 Water Polo

Ages 8-18 | All Skill Levels

Water polo combines swimming endurance with team strategy, making it one of the most exciting aquatic sports. Our program teaches fundamental skills while developing game sense and teamwork. Athletes compete in local and regional tournaments throughout the season.

What You'll Learn:

  • Ball handling, passing, and shooting technique
  • Treading water (eggbeater kick) and swimming endurance
  • Offensive and defensive positioning
  • Game strategy and team communication

Schedule:

Practice 4x per week | Monday, Wednesday, Friday, Saturday
Session times: 5:00 PM - 7:00 PM (weekdays), 10:00 AM - 12:00 PM (Saturday)

🏅 Elite Training

Ages 12-18 | Advanced / Competitive

Our Elite Training program is designed for serious athletes committed to competing at the highest levels. Athletes receive intensive coaching, video analysis, and personalized training plans. Many of our elite athletes have gone on to earn college scholarships and compete at national events.

Program Includes:

  • 5-6 practices per week with specialized coaching
  • Video analysis and technique refinement
  • Strength and conditioning (dryland training)
  • College recruitment guidance and preparation
  • Travel to regional and national competitions

Requirements:

Athletes must try out for the Elite program. Prior competitive experience required.
Tryouts held at the beginning of each season.
