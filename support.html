Support Youth Water Polo

Help us bring world-class water polo and swimming to more kids in Wesley Chapel and Pasco County.

Why It Matters

Water Polo in Pasco County

Youth water polo opportunities in Pasco County are still very limited. Your support directly enables us to grow the program, reach more kids, and give young athletes an opportunity they otherwise wouldn't have.

How Your Support Helps

🤽
Water Polo Equipment

Balls, goals, caps, and training gear — the basics that every athlete needs to learn and grow.

📚
Program Development

Curriculum resources, coaching certifications, and training materials that raise the quality of every session.

🏆
Youth Clinics & Events

Free or reduced-cost clinics for the community — making water polo accessible to families of all backgrounds.

🌱
Program Expansion

Growing the program means more sessions, more age groups, and more kids getting to experience this incredible sport.
