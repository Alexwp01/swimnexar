The Story

From National Team
to Your Child's Coach

Coach Alex competed at the highest levels of water polo — representing Kazakhstan at the Asian Championships and earning a gold medal with the National Team. That experience taught him that elite performance isn't born, it's built through disciplined, technique-first coaching.

Now based in Wesley Chapel, FL, Coach Alex brings that world-class perspective to youth athletes. His approach: meet every child where they are, teach fundamentals that last a lifetime, and make training something athletes genuinely love.

He also coaches the Nexar Swim Team, offering 1-on-1 sessions via Zoom or in-person for swimmers who want personalized technique analysis and college recruitment guidance.

Coaching Philosophy

How Coach Alex Develops Athletes

🎯

Technique First

Every drill, every rep starts with the right mechanics. Bad habits are hard to break — Coach Alex builds it right from the beginning so athletes never have to unlearn.

🧠

Game Intelligence

Physical skill alone doesn't win games. Alex teaches players to read the field, anticipate plays, and make smart decisions under pressure — the same mental skills he used at the national level.

💪

Individual Growth

Every athlete enters at a different level. Coach Alex meets each kid exactly where they are, giving beginners the confidence to start and experienced players the tools to compete.
