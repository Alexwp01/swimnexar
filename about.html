The Founder's Story

From Asian Gold
to Tampa Bay Pools

Alexandr Godovanyuk grew up competing in water polo at the highest level — representing the Kazakhstan National Team and earning gold at the Asian Championships. He learned what it actually takes to become a complete athlete: not talent, but deliberate, technique-first coaching from a young age.

When he moved to Wesley Chapel, FL, he looked around and found a gap. There were pools, but no real water polo programs. No coaches who had played at the international level. No place where a kid with zero experience could walk in and walk out a better athlete.

So he built one. Nexar Aquatic Academy started as one practice in Land O' Lakes — now it's two locations, a swim team, and a coaching staff that genuinely cares about every kid in the water.

What We Stand For

Our Values

The principles behind every practice, every drill, every season.

🎯

Technique First

We build skills the right way — fundamentals before speed, form before force. Habits learned young last a lifetime.

🤝

No One Left Behind

Beginners and competitive athletes train side by side. Every level gets real coaching, not just supervision.

🏆

Championship Mindset

We bring the mentality of elite sport to youth practice — not pressure, but the standard of focused, intentional work.

👨‍👩‍👧

Family First

Parents are partners, not spectators. We keep families informed, involved, and proud of their athlete's progress.

💧

Love the Water

Athletes who love practice improve faster. We make every session something kids look forward to — not dread.

📈

Long-Term Development

We think beyond this season. Our goal is athletes who thrive at 18, not just at 10.
