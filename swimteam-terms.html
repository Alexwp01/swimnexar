Swim Team Free Trial — Terms & Conditions

Nexar Swim Team · Wesley Chapel, FL · Last updated: January 2026

1. Purpose of the Free Trial Lesson

The free trial lesson is designed to evaluate the participant's current swim ability and water safety level, and to determine appropriate skill placement within the Nexar Swim Team program. Participation in the free trial does not guarantee enrollment or placement in the program.

2. Water Readiness Requirements

Participants must be able to:

  • Enter the water safely with coach guidance
  • Follow basic verbal instructions from the coach
  • Remain calm in the water

For deep-water sessions, participants must demonstrate the ability to float independently and perform basic propulsion. The coach will assess readiness at the start of each session.

3. Required Equipment

  • Swimsuit (one-piece recommended for younger children)
  • Towel
  • Water bottle
  • Swim goggles (recommended)
  • Flip-flops or sandals for poolside walking

No jewelry, sharp objects, or running on the pool deck permitted.

4. Inherent Risks & Liability Waiver

By registering for this session, the parent or legal guardian acknowledges that swimming involves inherent physical risks, including but not limited to:

  • Slips and falls on wet surfaces
  • Water inhalation
  • Physical fatigue from exertion
  • Unexpected reactions to water immersion

To the fullest extent permitted under Florida law, Swimnexar / Nexar Swim Team, its coaches, staff, and volunteers shall not be held liable for any injury, loss, or damage arising from participation in this session, except in cases of gross negligence or intentional misconduct.

5. Health & Medical Disclosure

Parents and guardians confirm that the participant has no undisclosed medical conditions, allergies, or physical limitations that would make participation unsafe. Any relevant health information must be communicated to the coach in writing before the session begins.

6. Coach & Staff Authority

Coaches and staff may remove a participant from the session immediately and without prior warning if the participant:

  • Appears physically unsafe or unready for water activity
  • Becomes distressed or overwhelmed in the water
  • Violates pool or safety rules
  • Behaves in a manner that endangers themselves or others

7. Parent / Guardian Presence

While parents are not required to remain poolside, all guardians must:

  • Remain on-site or reachable by phone at all times during the session
  • Ensure prompt pick-up at the end of the session
  • Be present for children under 12 years of age

8. No Enrollment Guarantee

Completion of the free trial lesson does not guarantee acceptance into the Nexar Swim Team program. Placement is subject to coach assessment of the participant's current skill level and readiness.

9. Media Consent

Swimnexar / Nexar Swim Team may photograph and video participants during sessions for use in social media, marketing, promotional materials, and internal training review. By registering, you grant Swimnexar the right to use such media without additional compensation or ongoing consent requirements. If you do not consent, please notify the coach in writing before the session begins.
